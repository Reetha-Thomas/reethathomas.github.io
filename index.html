About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIMB). I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from the Indian Institute of Information Technology Kottayam in 2024. My research lies at the intersection of mathematical modeling and data-driven methodologies, focusing particularly on air quality modeling, climate data analysis, and neural differential equations.

Prior to my Ph.D., I completed an M.Phil. in Mathematics from Bharathiar University, and M.Sc. and B.Sc. in Mathematics from Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam.

Research Interests

  • Fractional Differential Equations
  • Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)
  • Graph Neural Networks (GNNs)
  • Spatio-temporal Climate Data Analysis
  • Air Quality Prediction
  • Statistical Modeling & Quantile Regression
